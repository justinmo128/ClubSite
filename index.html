Welcome to the Ainlay Party Video Games Club website!

This club is about playing party video games, like Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, the Jackbox Party Packs, Gartic Phone, skribbl.io, and more! It occurs every Wednesday at lunch. We hope to see you there!

The club is led by Justin Mo, Oliver Lo, and Guangchen Wang, and was conceptualized on October 7th, 2022.
