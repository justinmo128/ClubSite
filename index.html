Welcome to the Ainlay Party Video Games Club website!

This club is about playing party video games, like Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, the Jackbox Party Packs, Gartic Phone, skribbl.io, and more! It occurs every Wednesday at lunch in room 344. We hope to see you there!

While this is a fairly lenient club, we do ask you to have respectful behaviour, good sportsmanship, as well as integrity. This club is for fun, DO NOT ruin it for others!

Most of our annoucements can be found in our Discord server or Google Classroom (not yet in existence).

The selection of which game shall be played each week will be decided by a vote on this Google Form.

The club is led by Justin Mo, Oliver Lo, and Guangchen Wang, and was originally conceived on October 7th, 2022 as "EPIC Part Video Games for people with tech devices".
